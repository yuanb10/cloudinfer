# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants in this project are expected to be respectful, direct, and
constructive. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and bad-faith behavior
are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent, but be precise about technical concerns.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Keep review feedback actionable and specific.
- Respect boundaries around security disclosures and private information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults
- Harassment or stalking
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration spaces

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the project maintainers through the repository
security or maintainer contact path. Reports will be reviewed promptly and kept
as private as practical.
